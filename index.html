📘 Student Portal

Students can view their attendance records, check subject-wise stats, and receive notifications for low attendance.

  • Login with student ID
  • View attendance by subject
  • Download attendance report

🛠️ Admin Panel

Admins and teachers can mark attendance, manage students, and view reports.

  • Mark attendance by class/date
  • Add/remove student records
  • Generate attendance reports

ℹ️ About the System

This system helps universities efficiently manage attendance using a centralized platform. It supports daily roll calls, statistics, and transparency between students and staff.
